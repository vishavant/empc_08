Why Choose SKAE ?

Skae is a complete Eco system for communication training + Practice Platform + Jobs. It's both the offline and online platform which provides spoken English training with personality development workshop with performance report to provide suitable jobs to the candidates.
Anyone from any part of the world doesn’t take classes to speak their native language. This is only because since childhood they have been listening to that language day and night, connecting all the actions with that language. This is the reason why without any tireless effort, they learn to speak and understand their native and regional languages. Understanding this concept of learning language SKAE has adopted the same methodology to make spoken English easy, even if English is not our native language. You only need to keep listening attentively in this LIVE G.D. classes and try to speak gradually. You will start feeling a difference in your confidence. This is the first APP in India where a moderator will be present with you at all times, who will also encourage you, give you a chance to speak up, and rectify your mistakes. We promise that you too will come out of your fear and reluctance to speak English.
